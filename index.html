Account Deletion Request

If you would like to delete your GameHub account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Account Deletion

Send an email to our support team with your account deletion request:

winzoneaiofficial@gmail.com

Please include the following information in your email:

  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  • The email address associated with your GameHub account
  • Any additional account identifiers (username, if applicable)

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon processing your request, we will permanently delete:

  • Your account profile and personal information
  • Your game progress and achievements
  • Any user-generated content associated with your account
  • Authentication tokens and OAuth credentials
Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Please ensure you want to proceed before submitting your request.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are typically processed within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.
